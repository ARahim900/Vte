## Design Context

### Users
Clinicians (doctors, midwives, nurses) and Ministry of Health officials in Oman who review
maternal **VTE (Venous Thromboembolism) risk** across health centers in the Suhar region. They
use the dashboard in a clinical/governmental setting to monitor screening coverage, risk-factor
prevalence, and center-level outcomes for 2023–2024. They need both at-a-glance KPIs and the
ability to drill into per-center clinical detail. Accuracy, legibility, and trust matter more
than flash — but the product should still feel current and engaging.

### Brand Personality
Modern and vibrant, yet credible. Three words: **trustworthy, current, clear**. The interface
should feel energetic and approachable (color is welcome) while never undermining the clinical
authority of the data. Avoid feeling like a generic startup template or a sterile spreadsheet.
The official brand is **Ministry of Health Oman** (moh.gov.om): teal `#1F5F7A` (primary),
dark teal `#1A3A4A`, navy `#1C3D5A`, gold `#C9A84C` (accent), Oman red `#C8102E` (national
khanjar emblem / alerts), sky-blue `#2A7EBD` (links), page bg `#F5F7FA`, charcoal text `#1A2A35`.
All design tokens in app/globals.css are bound to this MoH palette in both light and dark.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined, modern, vibrant data dashboard. Keep colorful charts and a lively feel, but tighten
into a coherent, perceptually-balanced palette driven by design tokens (not scattered hardcoded
hex). Strong typographic hierarchy with a distinctive (non-Arial/Inter) type pairing. Clear,
accessible data visualization is the hero. **Both light and dark themes are first-class** — a
working, well-contrasted dark mode is a goal, not an afterthought.

Anti-references: avoid AI-slop tells — same-sized gradient card grids, gray text on colored
gradients, glassmorphism-for-decoration, animated hero metrics as the only "wow", and the
generic "Flat UI" hex palette (#3498DB/#1ABC9C/#9B59B6…) used inconsistently.

### Design Principles
1. **Data legibility first** — clinical numbers must be unambiguous, high-contrast, and never
   sacrificed to decoration. Meet WCAG AA on all text and chart labels.
2. **Tokens over hardcoded values** — all color, spacing, and type flow from the design-token
   system so light/dark and brand alignment stay consistent.
3. **Vibrant but disciplined** — color carries meaning (risk severity, trend direction), not
   just visual energy; one coherent palette, not seven unrelated hues.
4. **Both themes, fully** — every surface, chart, and state works in light and dark with
   verified contrast.
5. **Hierarchy through type and space, not boxes** — vary rhythm and weight; don't wrap every
   element in an identical rounded gradient card.
